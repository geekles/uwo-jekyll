The University of Wisconsin Oshkosh — Where Excellence and Opportunity Meet

With 60 undergraduate degrees, more than 100 minors, 15 graduate programs and a doctoral program, UW Oshkosh is your place to find your path, grow your world, learn to lead and succeed.

As the third largest University in the state with more than 13,500 students, UW Oshkosh provides opportunities to explore your interests and passions.

UW Oshkosh is where you meet education, leadership, responsiveness, service and sustainability.
