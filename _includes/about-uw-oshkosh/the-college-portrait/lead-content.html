The College Portrait

Visit the College Portrait Web site for UW Oshkosh information.
The University of Wisconsin Oshkosh participates in the Voluntary System of Accountability, a partnership between the American Association of State Colleges and Universities and the National Association of State Universities and Land-Grant Colleges designed to provide consistent, comparable, and transparent information on the undergraduate student experience.

The PDF-based College Portrait provides information on the College to prospective students and their families, public policy-makers, legislators, campus faculty and staff, and other key higher education stake-holders.
