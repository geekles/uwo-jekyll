Spring 2015 Wireless Changes

New wireless access points have been installed in Kolf Sports Center, Student Success Center, Swart Hall, Harrington Hall and Albee Hall.

It is recommended that devices that connect to the campus wireless support 2.4 Ghz and 5 Ghz or '802.11a, g, n' standards at a minimum and '802.11ac' if available.

Any laptops brought for use should support both bands.
