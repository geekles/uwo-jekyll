Move-in Weekend

Welcome to the University of Wisconsin Oshkosh!

We are here to help make your Move-in Day be safe and stress-free.

Here’s what you can expect to find here:

  • Updates at uwosh.edu: Whether you are a new or returning student, our University homepage will feature easy-to-spot Move-In links to help get you up-to-date information from our Department of Residence Life.
  • Online maps: There are recommended routes developed to help get you safely and smoothly from home to your residence hall’s front door.
  • Facebook posts: Be sure to “Like” our UW Oshkosh Facebook page and stay up to speed on tips and suggestions from the University and the hundreds of other students, parents, faculty, staff and alumni who, likewise, like us.
  • Live Twitter updates: Follow us @uwoshkosh for real-time traffic, parking and Move-In Day information. We are already starting the conversation, sharing useful links and helpful tips with our followers. We have even set up a special Move-In Day hashtag — #uwomove.

    • Travel safely. We look forward to seeing you at UW Oshkosh this semester!
