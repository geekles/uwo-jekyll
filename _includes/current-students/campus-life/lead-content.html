Campus Life

As an undergraduate student at the University of Wisconsin Oshkosh, college life goes far beyond the classroom.

Whether you play on an athletic team, attend plays and concerts, or take advantage of service learning and career-building activities, it's easy to get involved.

If you live on campus, your residence hall will become your home away from home, and you will make friends with people from all over the world.

If you commute to campus, make Reeve Memorial Union your gathering spot. Grab a cup of coffee or a smoothie from Mi Taza or stake a claim on a comfortable couch in the commuter lounge or the Titan Underground.

At UW Oshkosh, you can:

  • Join the University's nationally recognized Model United Nations team.
  • Participate in sustainable practices, alongside the University's efforts.
  • Write for UW Oshkosh's award-winning student paper, the Advance-Titan.
  • Attend a Titan sporting event at the Oshkosh Sports Complex.
  • Join a student organization.
  • Become a member of a fraternity or sorority.
  • Play for an intramural or Division III athletic team.
  • Attend a campus wide concert or event.
  • Help your community by taking part in Titan Volunteer.
  • Take part in countless other opportunities.
