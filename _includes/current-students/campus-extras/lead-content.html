Campus Extras

From a state-of-the-art Student Recreation and Wellness Center to a contemporary student union and bookstore, UW Oshkosh offers many conveniences and services to enhance your life on campus.

Banking

The UW Oshkosh Credit Union, located in Reeve Union, provides full-service banking and four ATMS located throughout campus. Twelve banks and 11 credit unions in Oshkosh, with a number of branch locations, offer students additional options for banking.

Bookstore

The In addition to offering textbooks and school supplies, University Books & More sells a wide variety of UW Oshkosh apparel and accessories, magazines, DVDs and CDs. An authorized Apple reseller, students receive discounted rates on Macintosh computers and software.

Child Care

For students, faculty and staff with children, the Children's Learning and Care Center provides quality care at an affordable price. Care is available for children from two weeks to eight years old. The center also offers a four-year-old kindergarten program in partnership with the Oshkosh Area School District.

Copy Center

UW Oshkosh's Copy This! is a full-service copy center offering copying, faxing and laminating services. Conveniently located in Reeve Union, this center also offers UPS shipping service to destinations within the United States.

Corner Convenience Store

For quick groceries and snacks, small household items and personal items, students are able to shop on campus at the Corner Convenience Store in Reeve Union.

Dining

There are a number of dining locations on campus. Blackhawk Commons offers a variety of food items in an all-you-can-eat format. Another popular dining location is the Reeve Union Marketplace, which sells individually priced food items, such as burgers, pizza and pasta, stir-fry and sauté, subs, salads and fresh fruit. Other locations include Titan Underground, MiTaza Coffeehouses, Cyber Café, Scotty's in Scott Hall and the Crescent Café, which offers a weekly Taste of Nations luncheon every Wednesday.

Dry Cleaning and Laundry Service

UW Oshkosh offers pick-up and drop-off service for dry cleaning on campus. For basic laundry services, all residence halls have coin-operated washing machines and dryers for use by residents.

Library

In addition to cataloging more than 135,000 books and other library materials, UW Oshkosh's Polk Library provides the University community with access to a wide range of electronic information. The new Polk 101 offers students a study space that combines the best of a traditional reference room with a comfortable, technology-rich environment… and even a coffee cart.

Mail

For your convenience, UW Oshkosh's Titan Central, located in Reeve Union, is a full-service post office.

Oshkosh Sports Complex

Recent renovations have made the Oshkosh Sports Complex, the premiere facility of its kind in the state. This exceptional facility is home to UW Oshkosh's football, softball, baseball, men and women's soccer, and men and women's track and field teams as well as local high school teams.

Oshkosh Transit and Titan Transit

Students can use the Oshkosh Transit (city buses) or Titan Transit, an extended-hours bus service, free of charge when presenting their Titan Card.

Salon

Located in Reeve Union, UW Oshkosh hosts its own on-campus salon, Salon Shahnaz, which uses and sells Aveda products. As a student, you receive a 5-percent discount on all salon services.

Student Legal Services

The Student Legal Services in Reeve Union provides an attorney to offer students confidential and affordable legal advice, at just $5 a visit.

Student Recreation and Wellness Center (SRWC)

This state-of-the-art facility features multiple basketball and volleyball courts, an indoor soccer court, more than 80 cardio exercise machines, a jogging/walking track, climbing wall, aerobic studios, Cyber Café and many free-standing and machine weights. SRWC also offers group exercise classes, wellness programs, massage therapy and day or weekend trips. Due to segregated University fees, use of this facility is free to all students.

Technology Services

UW Oshkosh offers five general-access technology labs staffed by Academic Computing, in addition to departmental labs and labs in each residence hall. Also, the University offers free Wi-Fi-equipped laptop rentals.

Titan Card

Your form of identification on campus, the Titan Card, offers additional benefits to students. With your card, you receive free admission to regular-season home athletic events and have free access to Oshkosh Transit (city buses). The card also holds your meal plans and can be used as a debit card through U.S. Bank.

Titan Underground

Titan Underground in Reeve Union provides students with nightly food and entertainment, including karaoke, dart and pool leagues, live music, plasma TVs, Nintendo Wii, and Packer and Monday Night Football games.
