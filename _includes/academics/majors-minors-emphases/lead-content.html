Majors, Minors and Emphases

With 60 undergraduate majors and more than 100 emphases, minors and options spanning four exceptional colleges, UW Oshkosh offers a wealth of opportunities for personal enrichment, career growth and lifelong learning.

From the second-largest nursing program in the state to a highly respected business program, the University's affordable degree options provide students with nationally accredited programs, award-winning professors and an integrated liberal education that affords you the tools you need to thrive in our rapidly changing and increasing global society.
