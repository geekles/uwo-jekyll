Study at UW Oshkosh

Whether you are an undergraduate, graduate or nontraditional student, UW Oshkosh offers an array of quality, accessible and convenient degree options to match your unique aspirations.

Choose from 60 undergraduate majors, 13 master's degree programs, one doctoral degree program and more than 100 minors, emphases and options through UW Oshkosh's four colleges and the Division of Lifelong Learning and Community Engagement.
