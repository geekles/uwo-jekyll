Accreditation

The Higher Learning Commission will be on campus 05/21/2018 - 05/22/2018 to perform the comprehensive evaluation as part of the University's reaccreditation process.

UW Oshkosh received unconditional re-accreditation for the maximum period of 10 years (2007-2017) by the Higher Learning Commission (HLC) of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools (NCA) in recognition of its sustained improvement and the strength of its programs.

In addition, UW Oshkosh's College of Business, the only nationally accredited business school in the region. Fewer than 30 percent of the 1,500 U.S. business colleges earn and maintain this honor, according to the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB). The education programs of the College of Education and Human Services are approved by the WI Department of Public Instruction. The College of Nursing is fully accredited into the next decade by the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education at both the undergraduate and graduate levels.

For more information about accreditation and memberships UW Oshkosh holds, visit the Provost's Academic Affairs website.
