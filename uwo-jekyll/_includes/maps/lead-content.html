Driving Directions

Construction and Detour Information

As you travel to UW Oshkosh, you may encounter detours. Updated detour information for local highway construction can be found at the DOT U.S. 41 Project website.

From Appleton, Green Bay and Other Points North

Take Highway 41 South to Exit 124 (WI-76). Take a left off the exit ramp onto WI-76 S (Jackson Street) Continue and go straight through the roundabout at the intersection of Murdock and Jackson. Turn right onto Algoma Boulevard (a one-way street.)

From Fond Du Lac, Milwaukee and Other Points South

Take Highway 41 North to Exit 116 (South Park Avenue/WI-44/WI-91). Keep right and continue straight on South Park Avenue. Turn left onto Ohio Street. Continue straight. Ohio street will turn into Wisconsin Street over the bridge. Turn left onto Algoma Boulevard (a one-way street.)

Parking

Parking with a permit is permitted in any Staff/Commuter Student lot or in the Visitor lot (Lot 32). Lot 15, by Reeve Memorial Union and Blackhawk Commons, is easily accessible from Algoma Boulevard. Other parking lot locations on campus are listed on the Parking Services website.

Please display the parking permit in the driver's side window and do not park in a metered space, handicapped space or other restricted space.
