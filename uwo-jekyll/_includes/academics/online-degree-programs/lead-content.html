UW Oshkosh Online Programs

At the University of Wisconsin Oshkosh, students can take classes online, hybrid (which combines online and on-campus learning), in the evenings or on some weekends. View the UW Oshkosh Online and Hybrid Course Guidelines PDF

  • Bachelor of Applied Studies in Fire and Emergency Response Management (FERM)
  • Bachelor of Applied Studies in Leadership and Organizational Studies
  • Bachelor of Liberal Studies in Leadership Development
  • Bachelor of Liberal Studies in Liberal Studies
  • Bachleor of Liberal Studies in Organizational Administration
  • Bachelor of Science in Human Services Leadership
  • Bachelor of Science in Nursing - Accelerated Program (hybrid)

  • Masters of Science in Nursing - Clinical Nurse Educator
